Research EQ and IQ

What is Emotional Intelligence?.

Emotional intelligence is the ability to understand, control,communicate your emotions for example someone annoying you and in stead of snapping you realise your angry and ask them nicely to stop or remove yourself from the area to stop geting angry.

How is EQ different to IQ?

Emotional intelligence is where you can understand and interpret emotions from yoursdelf and others and being able to navigate interactions based on emotions. IQ is a way of expressing how capable you are at problem solving skills.

Why is EQ important?

EQ is important to have as being able to navigate feelings and emotions between you and others allows you to work productively with people who you might not like.
